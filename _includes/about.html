About

I'm a User Interface and User Experience Designer living and working currently in Helsinki, Finland.

I have a passion for human mind and how technology and designs can help and entertain us in our everyday life. I'm a curious problem solver and team-worker. I pay a lot attention to details, aim for simplicity, and get truly happy when I can help people with my designs.

When I'm not designing I'm crafting, dancing, and photographing. And playing video games. Because of course. And redesigning many of them in my head. Because it's kind of hard to turn the design side off even during free-time!
