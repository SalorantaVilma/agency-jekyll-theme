About

I'm a User Interface and User Experience Designer living and working currently in Tampere, Finland.

I have a passion for human mind and how technology and designs can help and entertain us in our everyday life, hence I chose UI/UX as my career. I'm a curious problem solver and a hard-working team-worker. I pay a lot attention to details, aim for simplicity, and get truly happy when I can help people through my designs.

When I'm not designing I'm doing aerial acrobatics, ballet, and photographing. And playing video games. Because of course. And redesigning many of them in my head because it's kind of hard to turn the design side off even during free-time!
